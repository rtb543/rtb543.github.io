By Car:

Take junction 27 off the M5 if travelling either North or Southbound.

If you are coming from the north, travelling southbound take Junction 27 signposted Tiverton Parkway. At the roundabout take the first exit towards Wellington. At the next roundabout take the second exit towards Willand and continue for approximately one mile. Take the first left after Hitchcocks Industrial park and continue down the lane to Uffculme Road. At the junction cross straight over to Selgars Mill driveway.

Please note that the venue has limited capacity for cars - please confirm via our google form if you plan on driving and who will be riding with you - we encourage car sharing where possible!

If you are driving, the venue requires that you place your name and number in your car window. There is no electric car charging on site.

Please do not drive any vehicles onto land or grass

Dress Code

There is no strict dress code

We’d love everyone to feel comfortable, colourful and themselves. The venue is set among the woods, so think

  • practical shoes
  • layers for the evening
  • anything you don’t mind getting a bit of grass or glitter on

Feel free to lean into fun, floral and woodland-inspired colours, or go full disco if that is your thing! Sequins, fur coats, face paint, fancy dress - all welcome!

If you’d like some inspiration, we’ve put together a Pinterest Board with a few ideas.

Packing List

As we have the run of the venue for the whole weekend, there’s plenty of space and time for activities and chill time. You don’t need to bring anything special, but here are a few things that might come in handy:

  • A swimming costume and towel (for the lake and sauna)
  • Warm layers, blankets, and sturdy shoes (for evenings outdoors)
  • Fancy dress or something fun if you feel like it
  • Any board games, musical instruments or craft materials
  • Your favourite snacks or drinks (there is a large communal kitchen space)

None of this is required - just some ideas to help you make the most of the weekend!
